Understanding the Law of Supply

This lesson explores the Law of Supply: as the price of a good rises, the quantity supplied increases, and as the price falls, the quantity supplied decreases. The graph shows a linear inverse supply curve, P = 2Q, where Price is on the vertical axis and Quantity is on the horizontal axis.

How to Use the Playground

  • Drag the dot along the supply curve to change Price and Quantity together.
  • Use the number field, slider, or +/− buttons—Price and Quantity always stay linked by the equation P = 2Q.
  • Watch the Revenue block update live as Total Revenue = Price × Quantity.
  • Observe how higher prices are associated with larger quantities supplied, illustrating the Law of Supply.
