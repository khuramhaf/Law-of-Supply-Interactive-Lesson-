How to Use This Graph

This graph shows how the supply of a product changes when its price goes up or down. It uses this simple formula:

P = 2Qs

The Golden Rule: When the Price rises, the Quantity supplied goes up! When the Price drops, the Quantity supplied drops.

Try these 3 tools to see it work:

  • Drag the Blue Dot: Click and hold the blue dot on the red line, then move it along the curve.
  • Use the Sliders: Move the thick blue sliders below the graph with your mouse or finger.
  • Type Numbers: Click inside the number boxes, type any number you want, and hit Enter.
